12-16句

People

Celebrity

Describe a popular/well-known person in your country.

You should say:

  • Who this person is

    • identity
      • 有名气的 ：well-received / go down very well
    • personal info
      • born in
      • university
      • competition / champion / medals
    • physical appearance
      • 胖 ：over-weight / big-boned
      • 瘦 ：thin / slim
      • 性格 ：extroverted 外向的， enthusiastic 热情的，versatile 多才多艺的，compassionate 有同情心的， benevolent 乐善好施的

  • What he/she has done

    • achievement / competition
    • how successful / awards
    • contribution -> donation, education
    • positive influence

  • Why he/she is popular

    • reasons / evidence
    • social media post

  • And explain how you feel about this person

    • Why you like/admire this person

Average person

Describe an interesting neighbor.

开头点题：Speaking of an interesting and creative/talented girl who lives next door ,the one that pops up in my head is my friend, Sally.

You should say:

  • Who he/she is
  • How you knew him/her
    • I firstly knew her was when I …
  • What you do together
  • And explain why he/she is interesting
    • Moving onto how I feel about her/ Why I believe she is interesting and creative….

place

  • Where it is
  • What it is famous for
  • How you knew this place
  • Why you think it is very interesting
